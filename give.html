Where Your Gift Goes

Every Naira Has a Purpose

We believe in full financial transparency. Here's how your contributions are used to serve our community and advance God's Kingdom across Nigeria.

⛪
Ministry & Programs — 55%
Preaching, worship, children's ministry, Ignite Youth, outreach programs, pastoral care, and conferences.
🏠
Facilities & Operations — 25%
Maintaining and improving the spaces where community happens in Okota and beyond.
🌍
Community Outreach — 15%
Food distribution, school support, and community programs serving Lagos neighbourhoods.
🙏
Benevolence — 5%
Helping members and community families in urgent financial need.
