First Time?

What to Expect

We want your visit to be comfortable, meaningful, and life-changing. Here's what a Sunday morning looks like at OCWO.

1

A Warm Welcome

From the moment you walk through the gates, our hospitality team will greet you with a smile and help you find your way. You are not a stranger here.

2

Powerful Praise & Worship

Our worship team leads vibrant, Spirit-filled praise that prepares hearts to encounter God. Expect contemporary and traditional songs of faith.

3

Children Are Cared For

Our Children's Ministry (ages 0–12) runs simultaneously with the main service. Your children will learn about Jesus in an age-appropriate, safe, and fun environment.

4

The Word of God

Bishop N.E. Moses or one of our gifted ministers delivers a practical, faith-building message rooted in scripture. Bring your Bible — or use the YouVersion app.

5

Prayer & Ministry Time

We believe in the power of prayer. After the message, our prayer team is available to pray with you for healing, breakthrough, or any personal need.

6

Newcomers' Connect

First-timers are invited to our Newcomers' Connect class where you'll meet the pastors, learn more about the church, and discover your place in our community.

7

No Pressure. Just Love.

We won't ask for your offering on your first visit. Come, receive, and let God speak to you. When you're ready to become part of the family, we'll be here.

Sunday Morning

Your Morning, Minute by Minute

A typical Sunday Service at Overcomers Church World Outreach runs about 2 hours and 30 minutes. Here's the flow.

☀
7:30 – 7:59am
Arrival & Pre-Service Atmosphere

Doors open. Our team welcomes you, ushers you to a seat, and soft worship music plays as the sanctuary fills. Great time to settle your children in.

🎵
8:00 – 8:45am
Praise & Worship

The service opens with high-energy worship led by our Worship & Creative Arts team. Sing along, raise your hands, or simply soak in God's presence.

📖
8:45 – 9:45am
The Message

A 45–60 minute Bible-based teaching from Bishop N.E. Moses or a minister. Practical, prophetic, and life-giving — bring a notepad.

🙏
9:45 – 10:15am
Altar Call & Ministry Time

An invitation to receive Jesus, rededicate your life, or come for prayer. Our prayer team ministers to individuals with laying on of hands.

💰
10:15 – 10:25am
Announcements & Offering

Church updates, upcoming events, and a time of giving. Newcomers are not expected to give. "God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Cor 9:7

🤝
10:25 – 10:30am
Benediction & Fellowship

Service closes with a blessing. Stay for fellowship, meet people, and enjoy light refreshments. First-timers are directed to Newcomers' Connect.

Getting Here

Find Us in Okota, Lagos

We are located at Okota Road, Okota, Lagos. Here are multiple ways to reach us.

🚌 By Bus (Danfo / BRT)

  • From Mile 2: Board any bus heading to Isolo/Okota. Alight at Okota bus stop and ask for Overcomers Church on Okota Road.
  • From CMS / Marina: Take a bus to Mile 2, then connect to Isolo/Okota-bound bus. Journey ≈ 45–60 mins.
  • From Isolo: Take a keke (tricycle) or bus to Okota Road. Journey ≈ 10–15 mins.
  • From Mushin: Head to Oshodi, then board an Okota / Isolo bus. Alight at Okota bus stop.

🚕 By Keke / Taxi / Ride-hailing

  • Tell your driver: "Overcomers Church World Outreach, Okota Road, Okota"
  • Uber/Bolt users: search "Okota Road, Okota, Lagos" and we are on the main road — easy to spot by the church signage.
  • From Festac/Amuwo: Via Mile 2 → Apple Junction → Okota Road. ≈ 20 mins (off-peak).

🚗 By Private Car

  • Parking is available on the church premises. Drive in through the main gate on Okota Road.
  • We recommend arriving by 7:45am on Sundays — parking spaces fill up quickly.
  • Our security team will direct you to available parking spots.

Questions & Answers

First-Timer FAQ

Smart casual is perfectly fine. Many people wear Sunday best (aso-oke, native attire, suits) but others come in neat jeans and shirts. God is far more interested in the posture of your heart than what you're wearing. What matters is that you come.

Our Children's Ministry (Overcomer Kids) runs simultaneously with the main service for ages 0–12. Children are checked in by a parent/guardian at the Children's Ministry entrance. Our trained volunteers ensure your children are safe, engaged, and learning about Jesus at their level. Teenagers 13–17 worship with the main congregation or may join Ignite Youth at Friday services.

Yes, parking is available on the church premises on Okota Road. Our security team will guide you. We recommend arriving by 7:45am on Sundays as spaces fill up quickly, especially during special services and conferences.

Bring your Bible if you have one — it's always good practice to follow along. If you don't have a physical Bible, the YouVersion Bible App on your phone works perfectly. Scriptures are also displayed on our projection screens. We occasionally give away Bibles to first-timers, so ask our hospitality team.

Absolutely not. We may ask visitors to stand briefly so we can welcome you, but there is zero pressure. You can come, sit at the back, and observe — and that's completely okay. We just want you to encounter God and feel safe here. Come as you are.

Newcomers' Connect is a free, no-commitment class held after the Sunday service once a month. You'll meet Bishop Moses and the leadership team, learn about the church's vision and values, hear how to get connected to a Home Cell Group or ministry, and get your questions answered in a relaxed setting. Light refreshments are provided.

We hear you, and we are truly sorry for what you've been through. Overcomers Church World Outreach is intentionally a place of healing and grace. Our pastors are approachable, our culture is non-judgmental, and our doors are wide open to those who are weary and wounded. Jesus specialized in healing broken hearts — and so do we. Give us a chance.
