When You're Hurting

Pain is real. Whatever you're carrying — grief, illness, heartbreak, loneliness, or the weight of trauma — God sees you. And so do we. You are not invisible here.

All Grief & Loss Illness & Healing Heartbreak Loneliness Trauma Rejection Betrayal
"He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds."
— Psalm 147:3

Your Path Toward Healing

There is no shortcut through pain — but you don't have to find your way alone. Here's how OCWO walks with you.

1

Acknowledge What You Feel

God is not afraid of your pain, your anger, or your tears. David cried out to God in the Psalms. Jesus wept at Lazarus's tomb. Bring your real self — not a performance.

2

Receive Prayer & Anointing

James 5:14-15 says to call for the elders of the church to pray and anoint with oil. Our prayer team ministers healing prayer every Sunday after service — come forward.

3

Talk to a Pastoral Counsellor

Sometimes you need more than a prayer — you need someone who will listen. Our pastoral counsellors offer free, confidential sessions. Fill in the form below to book.

4

Connect with Community

Isolation deepens pain. Join a Home Cell Group near you where a small group of believers will walk with you through the season. You were made for community.

5

Feed Your Faith

Listen to sermons on healing and restoration. Watch our YouTube services. Let faith rise as you hear the Word. "Faith comes by hearing." — Romans 10:17
