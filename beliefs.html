Our Foundation

What We Believe

Faith that is rooted in the Word of God, alive in the Holy Spirit, and proven by the testimony of changed lives. These convictions shape everything we do at OCWO.

At Overcomers Church World Outreach, we are a Pentecostal, Full-Gospel church. We hold to the historic Christian faith while embracing the fullness of the Holy Spirit's power for today. Our beliefs are not simply doctrines — they are living realities experienced by believers in our community every week.

Statement of Faith

Our Core Beliefs

Grounded in Scripture. Confirmed by the Spirit. Lived out in power.

01
The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — the supreme authority for all matters of faith and life. Every doctrine, every promise, and every instruction we follow comes from Scripture alone.

2 Timothy 3:16–17 · 2 Peter 1:21
02
The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Each is distinct yet co-equal, co-eternal, and fully divine. This is the God we worship and serve.

Deuteronomy 6:4 · Matthew 28:19
03
Jesus Christ Our Lord

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man — born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose bodily from the dead on the third day, ascended to heaven, and will return in power and glory.

John 1:1,14 · 1 Corinthians 15:3–4
04
The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit is a person of the Godhead — active, powerful, and present. He convicts, regenerates, indwells, and empowers every believer. We also believe in the Baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues.

John 14:16–17 · Acts 1:8 · Acts 2:4
05
Salvation by Grace

We believe all mankind has sinned and fallen short of God's glory. Salvation is a free gift received through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works, not by merit, but by the grace of God poured out at Calvary.

Ephesians 2:8–9 · Romans 3:23 · John 3:16
06
Divine Healing

We believe divine healing is provided in the atonement of Jesus Christ. God heals bodies, minds, emotions, and relationships today. We pray for the sick, and we have seen miracles — because Jesus is the same yesterday, today, and forever.

Isaiah 53:5 · James 5:14–15 · Mark 16:18
07
Water Baptism & Communion

We practice water baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith and identification with Christ's death and resurrection. We celebrate Holy Communion regularly as a memorial of His sacrifice and a proclamation of His return.

Matthew 28:19 · Romans 6:4 · Luke 22:19–20
08
The Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible, bodily return of Jesus Christ. He will raise the dead, judge the living and the dead, and establish His eternal Kingdom. This blessed hope purifies us and fuels our urgency to reach the lost.

John 14:3 · 1 Thessalonians 4:16–17 · Revelation 22:20
09
The Church & Kingdom

We believe the Church is the body of Christ — called to worship, disciple, and serve the world. We are not a social club; we are an army advancing God's Kingdom through prayer, evangelism, signs, and wonders until Jesus comes.

Matthew 16:18 · Ephesians 4:11–16 · Matthew 6:10

"And these signs will follow those who believe: In My name they will cast out demons;
they will speak with new tongues... they will lay hands on the sick, and they will recover."

Mark 16:17–18

Good Questions

Things People Often Ask

Are you a Pentecostal or Charismatic church?

Yes. We are a Full-Gospel, Pentecostal church. We believe in the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, the gifts of the Spirit (tongues, prophecy, healing, miracles), and the active power of God available to every believer today. Our services are Spirit-led, expectant, and lively.

Do I need to speak in tongues to attend or become a member?

Not at all. Everyone is welcome at OCWO, regardless of where they are in their faith journey. The Baptism of the Holy Spirit is a gift you can receive — and we'll pray with you — but it is not a requirement to belong to this community.

Do you believe in miracles and healing today?

Absolutely. We have witnessed healings, financial breakthroughs, restored marriages, and deliverances in our services. We believe Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever — and His power is available now. Come and experience it for yourself.

What denomination are you affiliated with?

We are an independent, Pentecostal Full-Gospel church. We are not affiliated with a specific denomination, but we walk in fellowship with other Spirit-filled churches and ministries across Nigeria and the world. What unites us is the Lordship of Jesus Christ and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Is your church welcoming to someone who has been hurt by religion?

Yes — and we mean that from the heart. Some of our most committed members came to us wounded, sceptical, or fed up with religion. We are not a religious club; we are a family that believes in God's grace, not human performance. Come as you are. You will be loved, not judged.
