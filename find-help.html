Life can be hard — but you don't have to walk through it alone. At Overcomers Church World Outreach, we believe God's grace is sufficient for every situation you face.

Our Support

How We Walk With You

OCWO offers multiple layers of care — from prayer and community to professional counselling referrals.

🙏

Prayer Ministry

Our intercessory prayer team prays over every submitted request. Prayer changes things — and we take every request seriously.

👨‍⚕️

Pastoral Counselling

Request a private session with one of our pastoral counsellors. We offer a safe, confidential space with no judgment.

🏠

Home Cell Groups

Connect with a small group of believers in your neighbourhood. Real life, real community — where you know and are known.

📖

Sermon Library

Access hundreds of messages on healing, breakthrough, faith, and family from our sermons archive. The Word has the answer.

🤝

Community Outreach

Our outreach team provides food, clothing, and emergency support to those in need in Okota and surrounding communities.

🔗

Professional Referrals

For clinical mental health, legal, or medical needs, we connect you with trusted Christian professionals in Lagos.
