Where Do You Belong?

From children to seniors, every person has a place at Overcomers Church World Outreach. Find your community, discover your purpose, and grow as a champion.

Ministry at OCWO is not a program — it's family. Our ministries exist to help you find a circle where you are truly known, equipped for your calling, and connected to something bigger than yourself. Every ministry is Spirit-led and purpose-driven.

Your First Step

How to Get Plugged In

Finding your place at OCWO shouldn't be complicated. Here's how we help every new person discover where they truly belong.

1
Visit on a Sunday

Come to a service and experience the community. No pressure — just come and encounter God at 8:00am in Okota.

2
Attend Newcomers' Connect

Our monthly newcomers' gathering helps you find your fit. Meet the team, explore the ministries, and ask all your questions.

3
Join a Home Cell Group

Real community happens in small groups. We'll match you with a Home Cell based on your area of Lagos and life stage.

4
Discover Your Gifts

Meet with a team leader to find where you can serve. Every member is equipped and deployed for God's Kingdom work.

🙋

Ready to Serve?

We have volunteer opportunities in every area — from welcoming first-time visitors to serving the community, running media, or teaching children.
