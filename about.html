Our Story

We Are Overcomers

A Spirit-filled church born from the conviction that God's power is available to every believer — and that every life touched by His grace becomes a testimony of overcoming.

Founded with Purpose

How It All Began

Overcomers Church World Outreach was founded by Bishop N.E. Moses with a burning vision: to raise a generation of Spirit-filled believers who do not merely survive life's challenges — but overcome them through faith in Jesus Christ.

What began as a small gathering in Lagos has grown into a vibrant, multi-location church with thousands of worshippers, impacting families, communities, and nations. Our Pentecostal roots run deep — we believe in the active power of the Holy Spirit for today.

Today, OCWO meets across Lagos, reaches souls online through our YouTube channel, and hosts landmark conferences including the Holy Spirit Fire Conference (HSFC) and the Jericho Experience — events that have changed thousands of lives.

What Drives Us

Our Mission
